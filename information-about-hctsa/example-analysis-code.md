## üíæ _hctsa_ datasets, and example workflows

Sometimes it's easiest to quickly get going when you can follow on to some example code. Here are some repositories that allow you to do just this. There are a range of open datasets with pre-computed _hctsa_ features, as well as some examples of _hctsa_ workflows. You may also check the [page of publications using _hctsa_](publications-using-hctsa.md)for example publications that have used hctsa‚Äîsome of these contain associated open code.
